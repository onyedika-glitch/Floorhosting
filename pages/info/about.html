Our Story

About Floor Hosting

Founded in 2024, Floor Hosting was built with one goal in mind: to provide a stable, secure, and supportive foundation for everyone's online ambitions.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, reliable, and secure web hosting services that empower entrepreneurs, small businesses, and creative professionals to build their online presence without technical barriers.

We believe that quality hosting shouldn't be expensive or complicated. Our mission is to deliver enterprise-grade infrastructure at competitive prices, backed by dedicated support that actually listens to our customers.

Our Values

Reliability, Security, and Customer Success are at the core of everything we do. We're committed to maintaining the highest uptime standards and protecting your data with industry-leading security practices.

Transparency and trust form the foundation of our relationships with customers. We believe in honest pricing, clear communication, and going the extra mile to ensure your success with our platform.
